Our Facilities

📍 Real-time Location-based Reporting

Public users can report suspected bad water quality directly from their location using GPS. Reports are logged for admin review.

📊 Sensor Data Monitoring

pH and TDS levels are monitored by IoT-enabled ESP32 devices and logged to the system in real-time.

🗺️ Heatmap Visualization

View a color-coded map showing water quality across locations, ranging from green (safe) to red (hazardous).

🔒 Secure Admin Panel

Only verified admins can log in and manage reported issues, verify locations, and remove outdated or incorrect reports.

⚠️ Health Risk Awareness

Clicking on affected locations gives users information about possible health risks due to water contamination.

🧪 Manual Verification

Admins can manually verify the public reports using portable sensors and update the map accordingly.
